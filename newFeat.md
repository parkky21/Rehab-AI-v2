1. Patient Analytics Dashboard

Recovery Monitoring: Real-time visual tracking of recovery scores (0–100) and average pain levels using interactive line charts.
Joint Mobility Tracking: Detailed progress bars showing range-of-motion (ROM) degrees for specific joints (e.g., knee flexion, ankle dorsiflexion).
Compliance Analytics: Tracking session completion rates and exercise consistency against assigned medical targets.
Exercise Breakdown: Visual distribution (Pie chart) of physical activity across Strength, Mobility, Balance, and Cardio categories.
2. Live AI-Guided Rehab Sessions
Motion Tracking: Camera-based skeleton tracking that analyzes body posture and joint alignment in real-time.
Live Form Scoring: Instant feedback on exercise quality (Form Score) with visual HUD overlays.
AI Coach Feedback: Context-aware corrective messages (e.g., "Keep your back flat," "Hips rotating slightly outward") to ensure clinical safety.
Automated Rep Counting: Intelligent rep and set counter that advances only when correct movement patterns are detected.
Real-time Biometrics: Live display of joint angles (e.g., Knee Flex, Trunk Lean) during the active movement.
3. Doctor & Clinical Management
Patient Triage: A centralized dashboard for clinicians to monitor multiple patients, with status badges highlighting "At Risk" or "Critical" cases.
Exercise Prescription: Interactive form for assigning specific clinical protocols and exercises (e.g., Post-ACL Phase 2) with customized sets, reps, and rest intervals.
Clinical Roadmap: Visual milestone tracking mapping the patient's journey from initial pain management to full weight-bearing recovery.
4. AI-Generated clinical Reports
Weekly Performance Audits: Automatic summaries of form accuracy, pain trends, and compliance.
Intelligent Recommendations: AI-driven insights that suggest program adjustments, such as increasing intensity or addressing compensatory movement patterns.
Communication Bridge: Features to instantly share verified reports between patients and their healthcare providers.
5. Premium UI/UX Experience
Unified Design System: A polished, "Medical-Premium" aesthetic using a clinical emerald color palette and clear typography.
Responsive Layout: Fully adaptive design optimized for both desktop clinical use and mobile/tablet exercise sessions.
Motion-Enhanced Transitions: Smooth, orchestrated animations when switching between analytics, live sessions, and reports.
